Everything you need to know

What is BetSweeps Partners?+

BetSweeps Partners is the official affiliate program of BetSweeps.us, a US-facing sweepstakes social casino. It operates on a cost-per-acquisition basis, providing tracking links, marketing creatives and a partner portal so you can monetize your traffic with a fixed payout for every player who converts.

What counts as a "qualified FTD"?+

A player referred through your link who signs up and makes their first Gold Coin purchase above the qualifying minimum. That event triggers your CPA payout. No purchase, no CPA — but also no revshare or carryover to complicate things.

Do you only offer CPA?+

Yes. BetSweeps Partners is 100% CPA: a flat amount per qualified FTD that scales with your monthly volume (from $90 to $160). No percentages, no surprises.

How and when do I get paid?+

Commissions are paid weekly via crypto, bank transfer or wire, with no hidden fees. Payments are issued after standard validation of your qualified FTDs.

Do I need a website to apply?+

Not required. We accept traffic from SEO, social, paid media, email, streaming and Discord/Telegram, as long as it complies with our policies and state sweepstakes laws.

What about restricted states?+

Tracking applies automatic geo-fencing. You focus your traffic on eligible states and we make sure conversions from prohibited geos aren't credited. See Eligible States.
